My Journey

With over two decades of experience in banking and mortgage lending, I've dedicated my career to helping individuals and families achieve their homeownership dreams. My journey began in Rhode Island's largest credit unions, where I developed a deep understanding of financial services and mortgage products.

Now serving the Palm Coast community as a Senior Loan Officer, I bring extensive knowledge in regulatory compliance and a commitment to providing personalized mortgage solutions. My approach combines professional expertise with a genuine desire to make the mortgage process as smooth and transparent as possible for my clients.

I believe that every homebuyer's story is unique, and I take pride in finding the right mortgage solution for each individual situation. Whether you're a first-time homebuyer, looking to refinance, or investing in property, I'm here to guide you through every step of the process.
