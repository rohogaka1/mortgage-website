Frequently Asked Questions

What credit score do I need?

Credit score requirements vary by loan type. Conventional loans typically require 620+, while FHA loans may accept scores as low as 580.

How much down payment do I need?

Down payments can be as low as 3% for conventional loans, 3.5% for FHA loans, and 0% for VA loans. Let's discuss your options!
