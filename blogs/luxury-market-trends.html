Luxury Market Trends in Palm Coast: 2024 Update

An in-depth analysis of Palm Coast's luxury real estate market, focusing on high-end properties and exclusive communities.

Current Market Overview

Palm Coast's luxury market continues to show strength in 2024, with notable trends including:

  • Average luxury home price: $1.2M (up 7% from 2023)
  • Days on market: 65 (down from 85 in 2023)
  • Increased interest from out-of-state buyers
  • Growing demand for waterfront properties

Premium Communities

  1. Hammock Beach
    • Oceanfront properties from $1.5M
    • Private beach club access
    • Golf course views available
  2. Grand Haven
    • Waterfront homes from $800K
    • Jack Nicklaus golf course
    • Gated community amenities
